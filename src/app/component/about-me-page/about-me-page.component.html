About Me

Hello! My name is Joshua Quizon and I am a 4th year Dean's Scholar at the New Jersey Institute of Technology. I am a computer science major with a heavy interest in web development. I love creating web apps because I get to see my code come to life rather than through some text in a terminal!

During my free time, I love to hang out with friends, play videogames, and taking pictures with my DSLR. I have also made it a habit to exercise at the gym. I strongly believe that mental and physical health is very important for STEM students, as the workload they receive can sometimes be very intense!

My current goals are to maintain a high GPA and work ethic, as well as improve my problem-solving skills. Although I believe I am a very capable programmer, learning is a never-ending process. As an aspiring software engineer, I have a moral obligation to perfect my craft and be a competent professional in my field!
